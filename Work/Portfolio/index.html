Frequently asked questions

+ - What’s the process for getting a website built with you?
I keep the process simple and transparent. We’ll start with a consultation to understand your goals and vision, then I'll create a detailed plan with design options. Once we agree on a direction, I’ll build the site, keeping you updated at each major stage. After launch, I offer support to ensure everything runs smoothly.
+ - How long does it take to build a website?
The timeline depends on the complexity of the site, but for most small business sites, it takes between 2 to 4 weeks from start to finish. I’ll give you a clear estimate once we define the scope, and I’m committed to sticking to the timeline we agree upon.
+ - Do I need to provide the content and images?
Yes, I recommend using original content and images for the best results, but if you don’t have these yet, I can guide you on sourcing high-quality stock images, and I can help with basic copywriting if needed. I’ll also give you tips on what content works best for attracting visitors and improving SEO.
+ - Can I update the website on my own after it’s live?
Absolutely. I build websites that are easy to update, so you won’t need coding skills to make changes. I’ll also provide you with a quick tutorial on how to edit text, add images, or make any other basic updates yourself.
+ - What happens if I need help after my website goes live?
I’m here for ongoing support. If you run into an issue or want to make changes, you can reach out anytime. I offer affordable maintenance packages if you prefer hands-off management, or I can provide assistance on an as-needed basis.
